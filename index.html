Research

My main research interest lies in human-centered computer vision tasks, particularly in 3D human pose estimation and human body mesh reconstruction. I believe that this is of great significance for artificial intelligence to better serve human society. Additionally, I am interested in infrared small target detection and tracking, FPGA real-time image processing, and its architecture, areas where I have substantial engineering experience.
